10100: Parameter identification: Quality factor not fulfilled

Info:
Data type:

Description:

Reaction:
The identification procedure is cancelled.

Cause/Remedy:
Motor - Identify parameter set:
- Motor lines too long or unshielded
Apply shield
- Unsuitable power combination of servo and motor
- Overload error
Decrease PIDENT_CURR_MAX and assign manually
Motor - ISQ-Ripple:
- Excessive noise in set current signal
Choose suitable filtering (SCTRL_TI_FIL)
- PIDENT_SPEED too high
Set low speed
- Excessive fluctuation of actual speed
Adjust controller settings
Drive - Inverter characteristic curve:
- Motor lines too long or unshielded
Apply shield
- Unsuitable power combination of servo and motor
- Overload error
Decrease MOTOR_CURR_MAX and assign manually
See Drive identification
