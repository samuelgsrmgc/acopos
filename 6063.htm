6063: Holding brake: External voltage on output over 24V

Info:
Data type:

Description:
Runtime error during closed holding brake. The voltage on the holding brake output exceeds the limit value 24V while the holding brake was closed (BRAKE_STATUS Bit2 = 0).

Reaction:
None.

Cause/Remedy:
An external voltage over 24V is applied during closed condition.
The voltage monitoring at closed holding brake can be deactivated by setting BRAKE_MODE Bit6 = 1.
